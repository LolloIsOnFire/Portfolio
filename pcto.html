Introduction to PCTO Experience

Over the last three years of high school, I had the opportunity to participate in the PCTO (Percorsi per le Competenze Trasversali e per l'Orientamento) program. This experience allowed me to gain hands-on laboratory skills and deepen my understanding of various scientific disciplines. The PCTO program included extensive hours in the fields of chemistry, biochemistry, and bioinformatics.

We learned both theoretical concepts and practical applications through this program. Each session was designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the subjects, combining classroom learning with laboratory experiments. This approach ensured that we could apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios effectively.

Chemistry and Biochemistry

During the PCTO program, I spent significant time in the chemistry and biochemistry laboratories. We conducted a variety of experiments that helped us understand fundamental chemical reactions and biological processes at the molecular level. Practical sessions included titration, chromatography, and spectrophotometry.

These practical experiences were essential in learning how to analyze and interpret data. They not only enhanced our technical skills but also taught us the importance of precision and accuracy in scientific experiments. The hands-on work complemented our theoretical studies, making the learning process more engaging and effective.

Exploring Bioinformatics

In addition to chemistry and biochemistry, the PCTO program introduced us to the fascinating field of bioinformatics. We learned how to use computational tools to analyze biological data, such as DNA sequences and protein structures. This part of the program emphasized the integration of biology with computer science.

This allowed us to explore modern techniques in genetic analysis and molecular modeling. The combination of theoretical knowledge and practical skills gained from bioinformatics projects has been invaluable. It has prepared us for future scientific endeavors, equipping us with the tools needed for advanced research and study in this interdisciplinary field.
